Apple Inc. is an American multinational technology company headquartered in Cupertino, California. Steve Jobs co-founded Apple in 1976 along with Steve Wozniak and Ronald Wayne. The company revolutionized personal computing with the Macintosh in 1984.

Tim Cook became CEO of Apple in 2011, succeeding Steve Jobs. Under Cook's leadership, Apple launched the Apple Watch in 2015 and expanded into services like Apple Music and Apple TV+.

Apple's flagship products include the iPhone, introduced in 2007, the iPad launched in 2010, and the MacBook line of computers. The company operates retail stores worldwide and has a market capitalization exceeding $2 trillion as of 2023.
